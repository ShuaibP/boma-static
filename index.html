Welcome to boma rewards
Where your rental journey meets recognition!

boma is a platform that rewards you for paying your largest monthly expense - Rent.

We understand that for many, home-ownership is a lifelong dream. As the number of renters continues to grow, we believe that your commitment to timely rent payments deserves to be celebrated and rewarded. That’s why we’re here to transform the way you view renting.

With our innovative rewards and rent reporting services, you can now track and showcase your exemplary rent payments while simultaneously boosting your credit score.

Our rewards program offers you access to a wide variety of discounts, incentives, and cash backs across a large number of retailers in the UK, making your everyday spending more rewarding.

Not only does this empower you as a responsible tenant, but it also paves the way for future lenders to recognize your reliability. Join us in redefining the rental experience – because every timely payment is a step closer to your dream home, a stronger financial future, and exciting rewards along the way!
