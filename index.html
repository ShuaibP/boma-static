Welcome to boma rewards
Where your rental journey meets recognition!

boma is a platform that rewards you for paying your largest monthly expense - Rent.

We believe renting should be more than just a monthly expense—it should be an opportunity. That’s why we're transforming the rental experience with innovative rewards and rent reporting services.

By tracking and showcasing your on-time rent payments, you can boost your credit score while unlocking exclusive discounts, incentives, and cashback offers from top UK retailers - saving on average £1300 per year.

Not only does this empower you as a responsible tenant, but it also helps future lenders recognize your reliability. Every timely payment brings you closer to homeownership, financial stability, and exciting rewards.

Join us in redefining renting—because you deserve to be rewarded!
